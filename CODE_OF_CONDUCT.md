# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- focus feedback on the work, not the person
- assume good intent and ask clarifying questions when needed
- welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, insults or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- bad-faith disruption of discussion or review

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove or reject behavior that does not align with them.
